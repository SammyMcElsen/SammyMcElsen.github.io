Manga, Manhwa and Manhua World

Hello fellow readers!

On this page, you'll get to find out what the difference between Manga, Manhwa and Manhua is.

We also want to show you a bit of the history behind those books, as well as Top-10 picks according to readers.

Manga

  • These comics originated in Japan
  • They are read from right to left
  • Manga are almost always printed in black and white

Manhwa

  • These comics originated in South Korea
  • They are read from left to right and horizontally
  • Manhwa are almost always in color when posted on the internet, but black and white when printed

Manhua

  • These comics originated in China
  • They are read from right to left
  • Manhua are almost always printed in color

*Not all information on this webside may accurate or up to date
