The history behind Manga

Here we will show you a little, how Manga came to be.

Manga first originated from emakimoto, these are illustrated handscrolls and are dated back to the 12th century.

During the Edo period (1603-1867), a book of drawings titled Toba Ehon further developed what would later be called manga.

It is suggested, that kibyoshi, picture books from the late 18th century, may have been the world's first comic books.

A big growth of artistic inspiration occured in the post-war-period. Thus a lot of earlier Manga-works depict wars or the times right after the war.

Boys and young men became some of the earliest readers of manga after World War II.

Between 1950 and 1969, an increasingly large readership for Manga emerged in Japan with the solidification of its two main marketing genres.

These two genres were sh≈çnen Manga, directed at boys and shojo Manga, directed at girls.

By the turn of the 21st century, manga "achieved worldwide popularity"

A few interesting facts about Manga

Here you read about a few interesting facts about Manga.

  • Why Manga characters have so large eyes:

    • This universal trend was started by Osamu Tezuka, creator of the Manga "Astroboy".

    Specifically, it sprang from his love of Walt Disney animation, especially the movie "Bambi".

    Because Tezuka's work is a foundation of contemporary Manga, this trait became a defining feature of its character designs.

  • The bestselling Manga of all time:

    • That price goes to "One Piece".

    This Manga sold over 500 million copys worldwide, as of August 2022.

    The Manga "Dragon Ball" follows closely on the 2nd place.

  • The right way to read Manga:

    • Japanese is read from right to left, so naturally, Manga would be read the same way.

    Because of this, the first page of a Manga is in western standards actually the last.
